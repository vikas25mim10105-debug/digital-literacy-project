# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

---

## Important Safety Tips

1. Never share your UPI PIN to anyone

2. Do not click on links or messages that you don't know.

3. Always check who is sending you a payment request before you accept it.

4. Use different passwords for all of your accounts.

5. Turn on two-factor authentication, for safety.

6. Do not put apps on your device from people you do not know.

7. Check your bank account. Look at your transactions often.

8. Try not to use Wi-Fi when you are doing financial things.

9. If someone cheats you tell the people at https://cybercrime.gov.in away.

10. If someone cheats you with money call the helpline at 1930 for help.
